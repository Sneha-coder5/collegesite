Courses We Offer

here are some list of courses and their detailed information.

Intermediate

Intermediate courses are for learners who have a basic understanding of a subject and are ready to move on to more advanced topics. They are designed for those who have a passion or an interest in a particular area but might need more support when it comes to technical and study skills.

Degree

A degree course is a 3–4 year course that leads to a degree from a UGC-recognized university. Degree courses are a defined, integrated course of study that leads to an academic degree, such as a Bachelor of Arts or Bachelor of Science. Degree programs may or may not require a student to declare a specialization. Some popular degree courses include: Engineering, Medicine, Law, Business Management, Arts and Humanities.

Post Graduation

A postgraduate degree is a higher-level qualification that will further develop your knowledge in a particular subject area. You usually have to have completed an undergraduate degree before you can continue on to postgraduate study.
